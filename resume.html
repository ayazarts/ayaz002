Ayaz's Arts Life Milestones

Ayaz Ekici has achieved remarkable milestones throughout his career as a fine artist. While working as a fine art teacher he organized art exhibitions every academic year, fostering a creative environment where students learn happily. The walls of the school are adorned with beautiful artworks painted by him. His art workshop was a paradise for the students’ study freely and relax mind. Beyond his teaching life, during the tenure of artist life, he has organized over 10(ten) solo, joint and group art exhibitions/workshops and every event were highlighted and recognized in online platforms and regional news lines. There all art events were environmental awareness projects and sustainable projects continue to contribute to the society in which people's happy aesthetic feelings can develop, with different perspectives that are beneficial to humanity and our world. With innate skills in fine arts and sculpturing, Ayaz passionately pursues fine arts as both profession and as a hobby where he meets his soul in fine arts.
