About

Ayaz Ekici is a talented multi-skilled fine artist of Turkish descent, born in1987 at Malatya, Turkey. He completed his high school education in Malatya. In 2011, he was enrolled to Atatürk University, Department of Sculpture in Faculty of Fine Arts. After completion of one year in sculpture education, he moved to Inonu University in Malatya for his graduate studies in Visual Arts in the faculty of fine arts studies.

Ayaz successfully completed and obtained graduation of Arts Studies in 2015. Later, he started working at Malatya Gökhan Ertan Vocational High School in 2016. In 2017, he was appointed to Şanlıurfa Turkey Atatürk School. He worked on many projects and arts exhibitions there.

Ayaz created an art workshop within the school. He worked on the development of the child and organised arts exhibition every academic year. Ayaz Ekici’s art exhibitions were highlighted and famed in all the social and news medias from 2016 onwards. During past years he has organized over 10 number of solo and join art exhibitions under different themes and all events were highly recognized.

As a visual artist uniqueness of Ayaz Ekici is skills and talents in using different medium such as oil painting, mural, acrylic, wall painting, graffiti & sculpture. He is mastered in mixing colours and find the best contrast in arts.

Also, he was the official artist of Sanliurfa Green Crescent Community Club in 2017 – 2019, 03-year period. He actively contributed many social and community activities in Sanliurfa city in Turkey. Specifically, applying of fine arts in wellbeing and positive approach of the community development. He was the keynote speaker of many CSR related activities in Sanliurfa, Turkey.

Further he extended his studies for another direction. In 2022, he successfully completed the associate degree in elderly care and psychological approaches of healing with arts from Eskişehir Anadolu University in 2023. He voluntarily worked in nursing homes and elderly care centers for 18 months.

In April 2023, he arrived Dubai, (Es Dubai Language School) and started a full time English Studies diploma aiming improvement of English proficiency. While studying he continues his artistic works in the Emirates. Currently he works on his arts aiming forthcoming Arts Exhibitions in Dubai. Also, he runs his own art workshop to enhance his art skills while learning UAE history, culture and social attributes His vision is to be an established well-known fine artist in UAE region and to contribute his art works and soft skills towards positive growth of the community where he lives in.
